About Me

It is I, Cuong Pham, and welcome to my humble site. What is the point of this site you ask? Well it is an assignment, Week 2 more precisely, and I shall be your guide to the world of Coding Bootcamp with HTML (and CSS) plus BootStrap. In this assignment, we shall explore the depths of CSS. Even now you can come to grips with its raw power. See how the text you are reading wraps around that delicious picture of grilled steak? That is the power of CSS. Surely, your mouth must be watering at the sight of such delectable website formatting. But wait, there's more! With CSS you can even add the amazing background image of a bunch of cubes! That is also the power of CSS! The true power of CSS shall come to life before your very eyes and I shall be your guide. Come, there is much to see but nothing to do.
